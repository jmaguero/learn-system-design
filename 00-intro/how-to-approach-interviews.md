# How to approach a system design interview

## The flow
1) Clarify requirements, 2) Estimate scale, 3) Propose high-level design, 4) Deep dive on bottlenecks, 5) Discuss trade-offs & failure modes.

## What interviewers look for
Reasoning, prioritization, and explicit trade-offs more than a single “perfect” architecture.
